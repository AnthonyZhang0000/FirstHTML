Reducing Impact on the Environment
How can we minimize the impact of computer device production on the environment? Be aware of how much resources have been extracted, use resources efficiently during the production process. Picture
Efficient Resource Use
    Increase the price of the produce so you don't need to extract as much Slowly remove resources that are harmful to the environment out of your product Use eco-friendly materials throughout the product
What are some ways to reduce the environmental impact of computer use? Watch how much energy you have used, have energy-efficient appliances and set a time limit and optimize your settings and usage. picture
Optimize your settings and usage
  • Use dark mode
  • Turn of notifications
  • Lower screen brightness
  • Enable power saving mode
How can we minimize the impact of computer device disposal on the environment? Recycle old technology, buy computers that are made with biodegradable materials. Picture
Old technology
  • Less technology in the landfills
  • Reduces consumption of natural resources
